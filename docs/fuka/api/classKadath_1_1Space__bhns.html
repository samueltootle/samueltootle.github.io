Constructor with an outer shell surrounding the bispheric region; stars are initial spherical.

Parameters
ttype[input] : the type of basis.
dist[input] : distance between the centers of the two spheres.
NS_bounds[input] : the boundaries of the various NS domains from Nucleus to ROUT
BH_bounds[input] : the boundaries of the various BH domains from Nucleus to ROUT
outer_bounds[input] : the boundaries of the various outer shell domains from OUTER+5 to ndom-1
nr[input] : number of points in each dimension
n_inner_shellsNS[input] : number of shells inside the NS

The various domains are then :

  • One Domain_nucleus, of radius rinstar1, centered at .
  • One Domain_shell_outer_adapted centered at .
  • One Domain_shell_inner_homothetic centered at .
  • One Domain_nucleus, of radius rinstar2, centered at .
  • One Domain_shell_outer_adapted centered at .
  • One Domain_shell_inner_homothetic centered at .
  • One Domain_bispheric_chi_first near the first sphere.
  • One Domain_bispheric_rect near the first sphere.
  • One Domain_bispheric_eta_first inbetween the two spheres.
  • One Domain_bispheric_rect near the second sphere.
  • One Domain_bispheric_chi_first near the second sphere.
  • One Domain_shell centered on the origin
  • One Domain_compact centered on the origin, with inner radius .
