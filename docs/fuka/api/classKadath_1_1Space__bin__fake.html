Detailed Description

Spacetime intended for fake binary neutron star configurations (see constructor for details about the domains used). By fake one means that the shape of the stars kept fixed to spherica. The radii of the holes are variables.

Parameters

dist[input] : distance between the centers of the two spheres.
r1[input] : radius of the first sphere.
r2[input] : radius of the second sphere.
rbi[input] : radius of the outer boundary of the bispherical part.
rext[input] : inner radius of the compactified domain.
nr[input] : number of points in each dimension

The various domains are then :

  • One Domain_nucleus, of radius , centered at .
  • One Domain_nucleus, of radius , centered at .
  • One Domain_bispheric_chi_first near the first sphere.
  • One Domain_bispheric_rect near the first sphere.
  • One Domain_bispheric_eta_first inbetween the two spheres.
  • One Domain_bispheric_rect near the second sphere.
  • One Domain_bispheric_chi_first near the second sphere.
  • One Domain_shell_surr centered on the origin.
  • One Domain_compact centered on the origin.
